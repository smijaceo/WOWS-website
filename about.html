How the Pack Began

Two years ago, Ryan and Anish were where most of you are now — losing money in expensive guru groups, getting mixed signals, and watching their accounts bleed. The "premium" communities they joined promised the world but delivered recycled strategies and zero accountability.

After losing over $30K combined in failed signals from other groups, they decided to build something different. Not another hype machine selling dreams, but a transparent community focused on real results and proper risk management.

"We started with just 12 people in a Discord server. No fancy marketing, no fake screenshots. Just two frustrated traders sharing what actually worked and tracking every trade transparently."

The first 6 months were brutal. Win rate around 45%, lots of learning, lots of mistakes shared publicly. But something clicked in month 7 when they partnered with The Strategy Factory for algorithmic signals. The community started growing organically as word spread about the transparency and real results.

18 months later, Wolves of Wall Street has become what they always wished existed — a place where traders actually help each other succeed instead of selling false hope.
